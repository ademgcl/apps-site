Privacy Policy

Dream Eleven keeps your data local.

Effective date: April 20, 2026

Data Collection

Dream Eleven does not collect personal data from players.

Analytics and Tracking

Dream Eleven does not use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking.

Accounts

Dream Eleven does not require account creation, cloud login, or sign-in to play matches or track progression.

Local Save Data

The game stores progression such as best scores, fans, credits, signature shards, academy rank, and mastery stars locally on the device so the single-player experience works offline.
