How the match plays

Auto Flow, Player Decisions

Footballers move and circulate the ball on their own. The match flows like a broadcast — you step in only when the moment matters.

Tap to Focus

When you see an opening, tap the screen. Time slows, the HUD sharpens, and you pick the action that turns the play.

Swipe to Commit

Swipe direction and length decide the outcome — a through-ball, a curling shot, a crunching tackle, or a signature finish.

Match Systems

Duel Resolution

Attacker vs defender clashes resolve with timing, stats, and swipe quality — creating readable, dramatic outcomes on every encounter.

Shot and Keeper Clashes

Shots trigger cinematic keeper duels. Power, angle, and spirit investment decide whether the ball hits the net or the gloves push it away.

Progression and Mastery

Persistent fans, credits, signature shards, and academy-rank progression. Featured mastery objectives with bonus rewards and perfect-sweep tracking.
