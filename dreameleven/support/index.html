Dream Eleven is an anime-style football action game for iPhone and iPad, built around auto-play flow with tap and swipe control for decisive moments.

Help Contact

For bugs, gameplay issues, or questions, email ademgcl@gmail.com.

What To Include

  • Your device model and iOS version
  • The match situation or encounter where the issue occurred
  • A short description of what happened

Response Scope

Gameplay: match flow, swipe actions, spirit meter, encounters, and progression.

Technical: launch problems, crashes, save data, and input issues.

Controls Quick Reference

During live play, tap to enter Focus Mode and slow time. Swipe to commit to a directional action — pass, shot, carry, tackle, or signature finish. Release a tap without swiping for the safer default action.
